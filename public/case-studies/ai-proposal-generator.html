AI Proposal Generator

Turning hours of proposal writing into seconds, without losing quality or consistency.
AI Product Status: Live ai-proposal-generator
01

Executive Summary

Writing tailored client proposals by hand is a sales bottleneck: hours per document, inconsistent quality between team members, and slow turnaround that lets warm deals cool. Zyverra Labs built an AI product that generates customized, professional proposals in seconds from a few structured inputs, so proposal writing stops gating the pipeline.

02

Business Problem

The problem
The proposal is often the slowest, most repetitive step between interest and a signed deal.
  • Each tailored proposal takes hours of manual writing.
  • Quality and tone vary between team members.
  • Slow turnaround lets warm opportunities go cold.
  • Senior time is spent drafting instead of selling.
03

Solution Delivered

What we built
An AI product that drafts complete, professional proposals from a handful of structured inputs.

It applies a consistent structure and tone every time, produces an editable draft in seconds, and removes proposal creation as a constraint on how many opportunities a team can pursue. Humans stay in control: the AI does the heavy lifting, the team finishes and sends.

04

Key Features

  • AI-generated drafts from minimal inputs
  • Customizable templates and structure
  • Professional, on-brand tone applied automatically
  • Near-instant turnaround
  • Editable drafts for fast human finishing
  • Repeatable quality across the team
