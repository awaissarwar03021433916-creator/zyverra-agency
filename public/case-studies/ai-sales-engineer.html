01

Executive Summary

Most website visitors leave without ever speaking to a salesperson, and those silent exits are lost revenue. The AI Sales Engineer is a complete AI sales platform that works the way a great sales development rep would, at scale and around the clock. It engages every visitor in natural conversation, answers their questions accurately from your own knowledge base, qualifies and scores them against your criteria, captures their contact details inside the conversation, and delivers a sales-ready lead, with full history, straight into the CRM. The result is an automated top-of-funnel that turns ordinary website traffic into a qualified, prioritized pipeline while freeing the sales team to focus on closing.

02

Business Problem

The problem
Businesses lose leads because website visitors leave without ever speaking to a salesperson, and qualifying the few who do reach out eats expensive sales time.
  • High-intent visitors arrive with questions and leave them unanswered.
  • No one engages prospects 24/7 across time zones and languages.
  • Interest is never captured, so there is nothing to follow up on.
  • Reps waste hours qualifying low-intent enquiries by hand.
  • Follow-up is slow and context-light, so warm leads go cold.
  • Static contact forms are abandoned and convert poorly.
03

Solution Delivered

What we built
An AI-powered sales platform that engages visitors, qualifies and scores leads, answers questions from a knowledge base, captures contact details, and stores every conversation, all orchestrated by an autonomous AI agent and managed from an admin dashboard.

Rather than a scripted chatbot, the platform runs an AI agent workflow: it reasons about each visitor, retrieves accurate answers, decides what to ask next, and takes the actions a sales rep would, capturing the lead and routing it to the CRM. Conversation memory keeps every exchange coherent, multi-language support widens reach, and an analytics dashboard makes the whole funnel measurable.

05

How the AI Agent Works

  1. Engage — greets every visitor the moment they arrive and starts a relevant conversation.
  2. Understand — retrieves accurate answers from the knowledge base using RAG.
  3. Qualify & Score — asks qualifying questions and scores intent and fit in real time.
  4. Capture — collects the visitor's contact details naturally inside the chat.
  5. Persist & Sync — stores the full conversation and pushes the lead into the CRM.
  6. Hand off — surfaces a prioritized, sales-ready lead with complete history to the team.
