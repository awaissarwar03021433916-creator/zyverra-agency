Business Systems Case Study

Employee Management CRM

Replacing manual record-keeping with one secure source of truth for the workforce.
Business Systems · Internal CRM Status: Live crms-tan.vercel.app
01

Executive Summary

Employee records, roles, and daily operations were being managed by hand across spreadsheets and scattered files, with no single source of truth, no access control over sensitive data, and decisions made on stale information. Zyverra Labs delivered a custom CRM built around the company's actual workflow: centralized data, role-based access, and operations tracking in one secure system.

02

Business Problem

The problem
Manual workforce administration doesn't just slow a company down, it introduces real risk.
  • Employee data scattered across files with no single source of truth.
  • Error-prone manual updates and duplicated records.
  • No access control over sensitive HR information.
  • Decisions made on outdated information with no visibility.
03

Solution Delivered

What we built
A purpose-built internal CRM, not a generic off-the-shelf tool forced onto the business.

It centralizes employee data, enforces role-based access so the right people see the right information, and tracks operations in one place, with structured workflows replacing fragile manual files. Security and data integrity are built in from the start.

04

Key Features

  • Centralized employee records database
  • Role-based access control per user type
  • Operations and activity tracking
  • Admin dashboard with management views
  • Structured create / read / update / delete workflows
  • Secure handling of sensitive HR data
