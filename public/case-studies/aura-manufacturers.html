Client Case Study

Aura Manufacturers

Turning an established manufacturer into a credible, conversion-ready digital brand.
Client Project · Web Platform Status: Live in production auramanufacturers.com
01

Executive Summary

Aura Manufacturers had a strong physical business but almost no digital footprint. Modern B2B buyers research suppliers online before they ever make contact, and finding nothing credible, they moved on to competitors who simply looked more established. Zyverra Labs engineered a fast, trustworthy web platform that presents Aura's capabilities the way serious buyers expect, and converts that research into direct enquiries.

02

Business Problem

The problem
For a manufacturer, credibility now starts with a web search, and Aura was effectively invisible to the buyers who matter.
  • No professional digital presence to communicate capabilities or quality standards.
  • Prospects researching suppliers found nothing that built confidence.
  • No structured way to capture and route inbound enquiries.
  • The brand looked smaller online than it was in reality.
03

Solution Delivered

What we built
A production-grade corporate web platform built around how B2B buyers actually evaluate suppliers.

Rather than a brochure site, it is a performance-first product: structured to communicate capability, optimized to load instantly, and wired to turn interest into qualified enquiries. The design system is brand-consistent and responsive on every device.

04

Key Features

  • Capability and product-range showcase for B2B buyers
  • Performance-optimized delivery and strong Core Web Vitals
  • SEO-ready structure for supplier-search discoverability
  • Lead-capture enquiry flow connected to the business
  • Responsive, brand-consistent design system
